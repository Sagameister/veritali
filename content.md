# Editorial Copy-Deck (`content.md`)

This document is the master copywriting deck and messaging architecture for the redesigned website. It takes the raw, operational, and transactional content of the classic digital portal—including active German property listings, verified client reviews, regional focus points, and philanthropic metrics—and translates them into a premium, bilingual (German & English) editorial format.

This copy-deck maps directly onto the modular layouts established in the Developer Playbook (`agent-v3.md`) and Visual Style Guide (`design-v3.md`), utilizing the **Space Grotesk Regular** display architecture and the airy **Plus Jakarta Sans Light** reading grid.
